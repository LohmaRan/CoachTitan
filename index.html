Our Products

Explore our range of coaching products designed to enhance your skills and performance.

Each of the 5 coaching applications were carefully crafted with insight from real coaches to meet their needs.

Track Titan

Designed to assist Track coaches in collecting and saving race times and splits. Can run 5 stopwatches at the same time to effectively time multiple athletes simultaneouly.

Field Titan

Designed to assist Field coaches in videoing and taking notes of their athletes performances. Provides a single place to save & view videos to assist at meets and practices.

Cross Country Titan

Designed to assist Cross Country coaches in easily calculating race splits. Provides a method to easily calculate live race scoring straight from their phone.

Workout Titan

Designed to assist coaches in effectively tracking workouts for their athletes. Provides a way to effectively analyze individual athlete workout performances.

Management Titan

Designed to assist coaches in their least favorite part of coaching... the paperwork. Provides an easy effective way to track uniform distribution and paperwork.
